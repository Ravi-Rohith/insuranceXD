ABOUT BLOCKBASE

WE ARE TOP

Blockbase is a transformative solution addressing the challenges of legal record management in India. Leveraging blockchain technology, it offers a secure, transparent, and user-friendly eVault system, managed through smart contracts. LegalVault integrates AI support for enhanced efficiency and security.

It uniquely leverages Aadhaar-based Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) authentication, simplifying user access while ensuring data security. This comprehensive system targets faster court proceedings, reduced costs, improved data integrity, and bolstered trust in the justice system. LegalVault not only streamlines document storage, access, and sharing but also empowers legal professionals and clients, offering a cutting-edge solution for India's legal landscape.
